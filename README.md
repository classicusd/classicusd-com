# Classic USD ($USC)

**The Fiat-Backed US Dollar for Ethereum Classic**

Classic USD is the definitive stablecoin for Ethereum Classic. US-regulated, fully backed, and redeemable 1:1 for USD.

## Overview

Classic USD ($USC) brings institutional-grade stability to Ethereum Classic. Every USC token is backed 1:1 by US dollars held in reserve at regulated US financial institutions, with regular third-party attestations to verify full backing.

### Key Features

- **US Regulated** — Issued by Brale Inc. (NMLS #2376957), a licensed money transmitter
- **1:1 USD Backed** — Every token backed by US dollars in regulated bank accounts
- **Third-Party Attestations** — Regular independent verification of reserves
- **Multi-Chain** — Available on Ethereum Classic (primary) and Polygon
- **ERC-20 Standard** — Compatible with all major wallets and DeFi protocols
